# Market Rankings

**API Key Required:** No

Query trending tokens, social hype sentiment, smart money inflows, and top trader PnL leaderboards.

## Capabilities

- **Social Hype** — tokens ranked by social media buzz (bullish/bearish/all sentiment)
- **Trending Tokens** — unified ranking across multiple signals
- **Smart Money** — tokens seeing largest inflows from identified smart wallets
- **Top Traders** — leaderboard by PnL over 7d/30d
